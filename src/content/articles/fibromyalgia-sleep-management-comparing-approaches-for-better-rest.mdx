<p>For managing sleep disturbances associated with fibromyalgia, both lifestyle modifications and assistive tools like mobility aids prove beneficial. <h2>Option A: Lifestyle Modifications</h2>
<h3>Overview</h3>
<p>Lifestyle modifications are fundamental in managing fibromyalgia-related sleep issues. This involves establishing a regular sleep schedule, creating a calming bedtime routine, and controlling sleep environment factors like light and noise. Dietary adjustments and stress management techniques, such as yoga or meditation, also play a crucial role in improving sleep quality.</p>

<h3>Benefits</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Non-invasive and natural approach</li>
  <li>Promotes overall well-being</li>
  <li>Can be personalized to individual preferences</li>
</ul>

<h3>Limitations</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Requires consistent effort and commitment</li>
  <li>Results may vary and take time to manifest</li>
</ul>
